Our Mission

The Open Data Council aims to coordinate student groups on campus to identify and obtain datasets that would benefit the Stanford community.

About Us

The Council was formed by the Stanford Open Data Project. It was formed after they realized that Stanford administrators would take dataset release requests more seriously if they were backed by considerable student support. The Council aims to be a straightforward organizing body to coordinate dataset identification and request efforts across student organizations.

Request a Dataset

Members of the Stanford community can propose datasets for the Council to organize around releasing. If there is sufficient support among the members of the Council, we can work with you to advocate for the dataset to be released.
